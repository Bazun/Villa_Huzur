The House

SLEEPING

The Shell House sleeps 12 and has 6 bedrooms in total.

Each of these bedrooms has a super-king size bed, full air-conditioning, with private terraces and ensuite bathrooms.

EATING

Our talented chef will provide you with a delicious breakfast and dinner every day.

If you have any particular dietary requirements, these can of course be catered for.

WELLBEING

The Shell House also has a dedicated gym, with a treadmill, spinning bikes and free weights, as well as an infrared sauna and treatment room.

Exercise instructors and massage therapists can be booked on request.

Location

Villa Huzur is above Oludeniz in north-east Corfu, set in a 3.5 acre olive grove, just 5 km from Oludeniz beach. Only 45 minutes from the airport, it has stunning views, and easy access to the nearby towns (and tavernas) of Fethiye, it has an ideal location.
